[TITLE]
Struggle for the Middle Sea
[SHORT DESCRIPTION]
An alternative campaign based on the original timeline. This campaign is the
struggle for the Mediterranean sea between the Allies and Italy from 1940
to 1943. The only difference is that France did not collapse in June 1940.
You are playing France or Italy, with the support of your allies.
